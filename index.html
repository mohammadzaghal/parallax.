Entering the Canopy

You begin above the clouds, drifting slowly over a floating forest. The distant sky barely moves when you scroll, while closer trees and islands glide past more quickly. This difference in speed creates the feeling of depth.

Each layer on this page has a different scroll speed. The further away it is meant to feel, the smaller its speed. The closer it is, the faster it responds to your scrolling.

Between the Islands

In the middle layer, floating islands and tree silhouettes slide across your view. Their motion is controlled with JavaScript using simple data-speed attributes. The browser reads that value and moves each element at a unique rate.
